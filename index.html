Hangman Game

Hangman is a classic word-guess game. It begins when you select a letter (by pressing a key). The goal is to guess what the word is, one letter at a time. If you figure out the word before running out of chances, you win! Play as often as you'd like and see how many times you can win. Good luck and Happy Hunting ;)
